# Meringue
## Time
| Cook Time | Total Time | Servings |
|-|-|-|
| 5 mins |5 mins | 1 pie topping |

## Ingredients
| Amount | Ingredient |
|---------|------------|
|3  | egg whites |
| 6 tbsp | sugar |

## Instructions
1.  beat whites until frothy
2.  add 6 tbsp of sugar 1tbsp at a time, beating after each addition
4.  pile on and bake at 325 for 15-20 minutes
