# Rum Balls
## Ingredients
| Amount | Ingredient |
|---------|------------|
|5 cups |vanilla wafers|
|1/4 cup| rum|
|12oz |chocolate chips|
|6 tbsp |corn syrup|
|1 cup |sugar|

## Instructions
1.  crush cookies in bowl
2.  melt chocolate and add to bowl
3.  add to bowl
    -   rum
    -   corn syrup
    -   sugar
4.  stir until smooth
5.  add cookie crumbs
6.  roll into balls
7.  roll in granulated sugar
