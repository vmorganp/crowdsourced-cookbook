# Frozen Mudslide
[source](https://www.acouplecooks.com/mudslide-recipe/)
![Image of example](https://github.com/vmorganp/crowdsourced-cookbook/raw/main/drinks/frozen_mudslide/images/image.jpg)
## Time
| Prep time | Total Time | Servings |
| --------- | ---------- | -------- |
| 5 mins.   | 5 mins     | 1        |


## Ingredients
| Amount | Ingredient |
|---------|------------|
|1 oz |vodka|
|1 oz |Kahlua|
|1.5 oz |Baileys|
|1.5 oz |heavy cream|
|.75 cup |ice|

## Instructions
1. Place all ingredients in a blender
2. Blend until smooth
3. Serve, optionally, add chocolate sauce on top
