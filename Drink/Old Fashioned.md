# Old Fashioned
## Ingredients
| Amount | Ingredient |
|---------|------------|
|1 pump|  simple syrup|
|2 dsh|  orange bitters|
|2 oz | bulleit bourbans|
|1 slice | orange wheel|
|1 | cherry |

## Instructions
1. Combine liquid ingredients in shaker
2. pour into glass
3. garnish with orange wheel and cherry
