# Tequila Sunrise
Source:

| Total Time | Servings |
|-|-|
| 5 mins | 1 |

## Ingredients
| Amount | Ingredient |
|---------|------------|
| 2.5 cups | ice |
| 2 oz | tequila |
| 4 oz | orange juice |
| 3/4 oz | grenadine | 

## Instructions
1.  Fill Glass with 1.5 cups Ice
2.  Combine in shaker:
    -   1 cup ice
    -   2 oz tequila
    -   4 oz orange juice
3.  strain into glass from step 1
4.  slowly pour in .75 oz grenadine
