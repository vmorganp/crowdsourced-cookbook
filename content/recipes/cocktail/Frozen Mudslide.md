tags:

---

Servings: 1

# Ingredients

- [ ] 1 oz vodka
- [ ] 1 oz Kahlua
- [ ] 1.5 oz Baileys
- [ ] 1.5 oz heavy cream
- [ ] .75 cup ice

# Steps

1. Place all ingredients in a blender
1. Blend until smooth
1. Serve, optionally, add chocolate sauce on top
